About Me

I am from Florida, but have always dreamed of living in the Pacific Northwest since I first came here in 2015.
The flat Florida landscape was never very inpsiring to me as a photographer and an avid outdoor lover.
The PNW felt like it had all I needed.

So, in July of 2022, I sold the house I bought at 19 for a bunch of money and sent all of our stuff here.
Meanwhile my amazing wife, two wild cats, and I drove 3,000 miles to the furthest domesic city from our home (the cats did NOT pull their weight behind the wheel).
